## What you see on a market page

Get familiar with the core elements that appear on every market detail screen.

### Question prompt

The headline describes the event being forecast. Each outcome underneath represents a possible result.

### Outcome cards

Outcome cards show the current price, implied probability, and the total volume traded so far.

### Activity stream

The activity panel highlights recent trades, orders, and resolution updates to keep you informed.
