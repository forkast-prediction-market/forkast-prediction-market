## Types of Orders

{process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} supports two types of orders:

<Accordion type="single" collapsible>

<AccordionItem value="market">
  <AccordionTrigger>Market Orders</AccordionTrigger>
  <AccordionContent>
  Executes immediately at the best available price in the order book.  
  Best for quick entry or exit.
  </AccordionContent>
</AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem value="limit">
  <AccordionTrigger>Limit Orders</AccordionTrigger>
  <AccordionContent>
  Lets you set a specific price.  
  The order will remain open until matched by another trader.  
  Best for large trades or when you want to control your entry price.
  </AccordionContent>
</AccordionItem>

</Accordion>
