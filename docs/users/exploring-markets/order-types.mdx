## Market orders

Market orders fill immediately at the best available price. They are ideal when execution speed matters most.

## Limit orders

Limit orders let you set the maximum price you will pay or the minimum price you will accept when selling.

## Managing slippage

Preview the quoted fill before submitting to ensure the order meets your expectations.
