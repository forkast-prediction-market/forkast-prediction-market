## Buying shares

Choose the outcome you want to back, enter the amount to invest, and confirm the quoted price.

## Selling shares

Hit `Sell` on an existing position to realize profits or cut losses. You can sell partially or close the full amount.

## Reviewing orders before submission

Always double-check the total cost, fees, and expected return before signing the transaction in your wallet.
