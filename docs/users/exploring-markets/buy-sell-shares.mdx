## Buying Shares

When you buy shares of an outcome, you are betting that the event will resolve **true**.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h3 className="!mt-0">Select an outcome</h3>
    <p>

      other available outcome you want to back.
    </p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h3 className="!mt-0">Choose order type</h3>
    <p>

## Selling Shares

You can sell your shares at any time before resolution:

- Sell via **Market Order** to exit instantly  
- Place a **Limit Order** to try to sell at a better price

When the market resolves, all remaining shares are automatically settled based on the winning outcome.
