## What you’ll see

- **Positions Value** – current value of open shares  
- **Profit/Loss** – realized + unrealized PnL  
- **Volume Traded** & **Markets Traded** – lifetime activity  
- **Tabs**: *Positions* and *Activity* (latest buys/sells with amounts and timestamps)
