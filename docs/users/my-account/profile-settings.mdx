## Profile

Update how your account appears across {process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME}.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Upload an avatar

    Click <strong>Upload</strong> and choose a JPG/PNG/WEBP image up to 5 MB to
    personalize your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Update details

    Edit your <strong>Email</strong> and <strong>Username</strong>, then press
    <strong>Save changes</strong> to apply.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Preview the public page

    Use <strong>View public profile</strong> to see how your activity and stats
    appear to other users.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips
- Usernames are unique. If a name is taken, try a variation.
- Keep a recognizable avatar so friends can find you easily.
