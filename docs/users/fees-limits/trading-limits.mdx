## Deposit & Withdrawal Limits

Limits may apply depending on your account setup or payment provider:

- **Crypto deposits** → No fixed limits, but subject to blockchain network fees.
- **Fiat on-ramps** → Provider-specific daily or monthly limits.
- **Withdrawals** → Typically processed without restrictions, except for compliance checks if required.

---

## Trading Limits

- Minimum order size: **$1**
- Maximum order size: depends on liquidity in each market.
- Orders cannot exceed your **available balance**.

---

## CSV & History Exports

You can export your full account history, including deposits, withdrawals, and trades, in **CSV format**.
Go to **My Account > History > Export CSV**.
