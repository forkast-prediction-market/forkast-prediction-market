## Scenario 1: Market order

You buy shares worth $100 at the taker rate of 1%. The total fee is $1.00, shown before confirmation.

## Scenario 2: Limit order fill

A limit order for $250 executes at the maker rebate of 0.2%, crediting $0.50 back to your balance.

## Scenario 3: Partial fills

If your order fills in multiple parts, fees are assessed on each fill and summed in the transaction receipt.
