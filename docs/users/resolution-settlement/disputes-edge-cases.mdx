## Raising a dispute

If you disagree with a resolution, submit a dispute within the designated challenge window.

## Community review

Disputed markets are escalated to community moderators who examine evidence before issuing a final decision.

## Resolution outcomes

Final decisions may uphold the original result or reverse it. All decisions are logged for full transparency.
