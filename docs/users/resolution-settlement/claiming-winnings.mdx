## Claiming Your Rewards

Once a market resolves, holders of winning shares can claim their payout.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Go to Portfolio

    Navigate to <strong>My Account → Portfolio</strong> and look for markets with
    a <strong>Claim</strong> button next to your winning position.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Claim winnings

    Click <strong>Claim</strong> to send the payout transaction. Your balance
    updates as soon as the transaction confirms on-chain.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Withdraw or reuse

    Use the credited funds to enter new markets or withdraw to your connected
    wallet whenever you like.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Example

- You buy 50 “Yes” shares at $0.30 → cost $15
- Market resolves **Yes** → each share pays $1 → you receive $50
- Profit = $35 (minus fees)
