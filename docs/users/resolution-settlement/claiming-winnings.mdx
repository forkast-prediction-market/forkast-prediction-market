## Automatic claim prompts

When a market resolves in your favor, the app highlights the claim action in your notifications and portfolio.

## Fees on claims

Claiming does not introduce additional trading fees, but you still pay network gas costs for the transaction.

## Tax considerations

Track claims along with your transaction history to simplify reporting and compliance.
