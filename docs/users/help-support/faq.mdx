<AccordionItem value="fees">
  <AccordionTrigger>What fees do I pay when I trade?</AccordionTrigger>
  <AccordionContent>
  Each trade includes a **protocol fee (1%)** and a **site fee (1%)**.  
  Part of the site fee may be shared with affiliates.  
  All fees are displayed before you confirm a trade.
  </AccordionContent>
</AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem value="claim">
  <AccordionTrigger>How do I claim my winnings?</AccordionTrigger>
  <AccordionContent>
  Go to **My Account > Portfolio** and click the **Claim** button next to resolved markets.  
  Your winnings are instantly added to your balance.
  </AccordionContent>
</AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem value="deposit">
  <AccordionTrigger>How long do deposits take?</AccordionTrigger>
  <AccordionContent>
  - **Crypto deposits**: usually confirmed within minutes (depending on network congestion).  
  - **Fiat on-ramps**: may take several hours or up to 2 business days, depending on your provider.
  </AccordionContent>
</AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem value="dispute">
  <AccordionTrigger>What if I disagree with a market resolution?</AccordionTrigger>
  <AccordionContent>
  You can file a dispute during the dispute window shown in the market page.  
  Disputes require a small stake and must include verifiable evidence.
  </AccordionContent>
</AccordionItem>

<AccordionItem value="withdraw">
  <AccordionTrigger>Are there withdrawal limits?</AccordionTrigger>
  <AccordionContent>
  Crypto withdrawals are generally unlimited.  
  Fiat withdrawals may be subject to provider-specific limits.  
  All withdrawal requests are processed promptly.
  </AccordionContent>
</AccordionItem>
