## Code of conduct

Treat other users respectfully and avoid manipulating markets through misinformation or collusion.

## Market listing criteria

Only legitimate, verifiable events are listed. Proposed markets undergo moderation before going live.

## Enforcement actions

Breaking rules may result in account suspensions or forfeiture of ill-gotten gains.
