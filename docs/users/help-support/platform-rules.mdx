## Community Guidelines

{process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} is built around open participation.  
To keep the community safe and fair:

- Be respectful in all discussions  
- Share accurate information  
- Avoid spam or manipulative activity  
- Report suspicious behavior to moderators

---

## Moderation Actions

Violations of the rules may result in:

- Temporary mute or channel restriction  
- Removal of content that misleads or harms other users  
- Account suspension for repeated or severe violations

---

## Transparency

All moderation actions aim to protect the community and ensure fair markets.  
For questions, contact moderators via Discord or email support.
