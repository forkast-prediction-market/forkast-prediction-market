## Email or Google Sign-Up

Signing up for Forkast with your email address or Google account is quick, simple, and secure.

## Crypto Wallet Sign-Up

Forkast supports most crypto wallets, including MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and others via WalletConnect.
