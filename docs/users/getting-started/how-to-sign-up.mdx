## Email or Google Sign-Up

Signing up for {process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} with your email address or Google account is quick, simple, and secure, just click on Sign Up button and choose "Continue with Google" option. You can also choose X, Discord, Farcaster, GitHub, Apple or Facebook as social login providers.

## Crypto Wallet Sign-Up

{process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} supports most crypto wallets, including MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and others via Reown (formerly WalletConnect).
