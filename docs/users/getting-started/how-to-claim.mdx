## Claim workflow

Learn how to release your winnings after a market resolves.

### 1. Confirm the market resolution

Open the market details page and verify that the outcome is marked as resolved.

### 2. Queue a claim transaction

Select the outcome you backed and click `Claim`. The app prepares a transaction for your connected wallet.

### 3. Sign and wait for confirmation

Approve the transaction in your wallet. Funds appear in your balance after the transaction finalizes.

> Need more help? Visit the dedicated resolution and settlement section for advanced scenarios.
