## Claim workflow

<Callout type="info" title="Before you start">
  Ensure your wallet holds a little extra MATIC for gas so the claim transaction can
  complete without delays.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h3 className="!mt-0">Confirm the market resolution</h3>
    <p>
      On {process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME}, open the market page and verify the
      outcome you backed shows the resolved badge.
    </p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h3 className="!mt-0">Queue a claim transaction</h3>
    <p>
      Click <code>Claim</code> on the winning outcome. The interface previews your
      expected payout and fees before you sign in your wallet.
    </p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h3 className="!mt-0">Sign and wait for confirmation</h3>
    <p>
      Approve the transaction in the connected wallet. Once the network confirms,
      the winnings land in your available balance automatically.
    </p>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Need more help?">
  Explore the Resolution &amp; Settlement section for dispute steps and edge-case
  guidance if your claim requires additional review.
</Callout>
